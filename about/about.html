About ShipDecKK

Discover the story, mission, and values behind the maritime industry's premier ship dealership

Our History

Founded in 2020, ShipDecKK emerged as a revolutionary force in the maritime industry. What began as a modest venture with a handful of premium vessels quickly evolved into a leading ship dealership known for excellence and innovation.

In just a few years, we expanded from our initial focus on luxury yachts to include cargo ships and luxury liners, establishing a comprehensive portfolio that serves diverse maritime needs across the globe. Our rapid growth is a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Today, ShipDecKK stands as an industry leader with an impressive fleet of over 200 vessels delivered to satisfied clients in more than 50 countries. Our journey continues as we strive to redefine maritime standards and deliver exceptional vessels that combine performance, elegance, and reliability.
